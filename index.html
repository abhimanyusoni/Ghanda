Client Requirement

Client is based out of Australia and already had an online web presence which was built in old technology and UI/UX was a major challenge along with poor performance. Solution was to use custom design based Magento 2 technology which can be customized to full fill client’s business needs. Client wanted to include the latest eCommerce features custom search, instafeed, promo code addition and most importantly a sleek UI/UX with easy shopping experience. Order tracking records were missing in the existing website & boosting all over performance was their ultimate goal.
